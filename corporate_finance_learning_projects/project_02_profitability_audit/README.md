Project 1 — Corporate Finance: Profitability Audit
Executive Summary

This project simulates a performance audit for a logistics company. The objective was to diagnose why Net Income dropped by 36% despite a 29% increase in Revenue.
Core Concepts Mastered

- Vertical Analysis (Common Sizing): Converting currency amounts into percentages of Revenue to identify structural cost shifts.
- Margin Analysis: Calculating Gross, Operating, and Net Margins to locate where profitability "leaks" occur.
- Operating Leverage: Understanding the relationship between Revenue growth and Expense growth (e.g., discovering that Personnel costs grew at 40% vs. Sales at 29%).
- Financial Health Indicators: * EBITDA (EBE): Evaluating core operational cash flow.
- Interest Coverage: Analyzing the impact of debt on the bottom line.
- Real Returns: Factoring in Inflation to determine if the company is creating or destroying shareholder value.
