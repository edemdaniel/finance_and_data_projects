Project 1 — Corporate Finance Fundamentals
Income Statement & Profitability Analysis

Objective

The objective of this project is to understand how a company transforms revenue into profit by analyzing the main components of the Income Statement (Compte de résultat).

Through this exercise, the goal is to practice the calculation and interpretation of key financial indicators used in corporate finance and Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A), such as:

- Gross Profit (Marge brute)
- Gross Margin
- Operating Profit (Résultat d'exploitation)
- Operating Margin
- Net Profit (Résultat net)
- Net Profit Margin

The project simulates the work of a Junior Financial Analyst asked to review a company's financial performance.
